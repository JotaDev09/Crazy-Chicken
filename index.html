While walking through the dessert of Mexico, you can pick up Coins and Salsabottles. Attention: The chickens of Mexico do not like you! If they touch you, you will get hurt. To avoid any damage, you can jump over the chickens. At the end of your journey, El Pollo Loco awaits you. To defeat her, you can use your bottles.
